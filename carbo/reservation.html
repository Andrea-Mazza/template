Reservations & Hours

We are a full service restaurant and no longer a BYOB. We know many of you have special bottles of wine that you would love to enjoy with Chef Campos' cuisine. Therefore, for the time being and while we grow our wine list, we will continue to offer guests the opportunity to pay a $25 corkage for the first bottle. Second bottle $35, third bottle $45, etc.

We accept reservations 1 month prior to the desired numerical date. You can after 11:00 am EST Tuesday through Sunday to make your reservations or through Resy.com.
